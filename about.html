About UiTM Student e-Profile

The UiTM Student e-Profile is an innovative platform designed to help students showcase their academic journey, personal achievements, and professional aspirations. Through this portal, students can create a personalized profile, add relevant information, and maintain a digital portfolio for future academic and career opportunities.

Whether you are a prospective student, a current student, or an alumni, the e-Profile system is designed to help you manage your academic information effectively. This platform helps students stand out by providing a digital version of their profile, which can be shared with potential employers, faculty, and universities across the globe.

Vision

To create a comprehensive and accessible digital platform where UiTM students can manage their academic, personal, and professional profiles, ensuring easy access to potential opportunities and making their achievements visible to the global community.

Mission

To provide UiTM students with a user-friendly, secure, and reliable platform that allows them to build and maintain their academic and professional profiles, while offering tools for managing their educational and career goals.

Objectives

  • 1. Profile Management: Allow students to create and manage a comprehensive digital profile that highlights their academic, extracurricular, and personal achievements.
  • 2. Accessibility and Connectivity: Ensure students have easy access to their profiles anytime, anywhere, fostering a sense of connectivity across institutions and employers.
  • 3. Career Readiness: Equip students with a digital portfolio that can be shared with employers, faculty, and other professionals to enhance career opportunities and academic growth.

Features of UiTM Student e-Profile

Student Profiles

Create and manage your personalized student profile with your academic achievements, extracurricular activities, and personal details.

Easy to Access

Access your profile from anywhere and anytime using the platform, ensuring convenience for students and faculty alike.

Secure & Confidential

Your data is stored securely and can be accessed only by you and authorized personnel, keeping your academic information private.
