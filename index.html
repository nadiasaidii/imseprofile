Research Data

CHART 1 (GPA CHART)

This bar chart displays the average GPA for five different departments: FBM, MCMS, CCA, KPPIM, and FSG. The GPAs range from 3.2 to 3.9, with KPPIM having the highest GPA (3.9) and CCA having the lowest (3.2). This suggests that, on average, students in KPPIM perform better academically compared to those in CCA, while the other departments (FBM, MCMS, FSG) fall in between.

This Chart consists of 25% of UiTM Students.

25%
CHART 2 (STUDY HOURS CHART)

This line chart shows the number of study hours per week for the same five departments. The study hours range from 1.5 hours (MCMS and CCA) to 4 hours (FSG). FSG students spend the most time studying, while MCMS and CCA students spend the least amount of time studying. This data suggests that there may be a correlation between study hours and academic performance, particularly for FSG students.

This Chart consists of 35% of UiTM Students.

35%
CHART 3 (LEVELS OF STUDY CHART)

This pie chart represents the distribution of students across different levels of study (PhD, Master’s, Degree, and Diploma). Degree-level students form the largest group with 3,500 students, followed by Diploma students (4,230). Master’s and PhD students are less prevalent, with 3,000 and 1,005 students, respectively. This indicates that the majority of students are pursuing undergraduate degrees or diplomas.

This Chart consists of 55% of UiTM Students.
