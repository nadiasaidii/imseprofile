Policy of UiTM Student e-Profile

We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use the UiTM Student e-Profile platform. By using this platform, you agree to the collection and use of your data as described in this policy.

  • 1. Data Collection

    We collect personal information such as your name, student ID, email address, program, and academic history when you create your e-Profile. We may also collect additional information provided by you voluntarily.


  • 2. Data Usage

    The information collected will be used to create and maintain your student profile, facilitate communication, and enhance the functionality of the e-Profile platform. We may use your data to personalize your experience on the platform and improve our services.


  • 3. Data Protection

    We take reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no data transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your personal information.

    You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your login credentials and all activities that occur under your account. You agree to notify us immediately if you suspect any unauthorized access to your account.

    You agree to use the platform only for lawful purposes and in a manner that does not violate any applicable laws or regulations. You are prohibited from uploading or sharing any inappropriate or offensive content on the platform.

    We reserve the right to modify, suspend, or terminate the services provided by the UiTM Student e-Profile platform at any time without prior notice. Any changes to the platform will be communicated to users accordingly.
